How to install:-

Copy homeRepath.py to Documents/maya/2018/scripts

Open maya 

Open maya script editor new python tab

Paste:-

import homeRepath
homeRepath.openFileAndRemapRefs()


Highlight and drag to shelf to make button. Right click and click folder icon next to icon space.
Browse to Repath_external_Icon file.

Now you can repath your scene references at the click of a button.
